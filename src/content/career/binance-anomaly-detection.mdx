## context

Internship in the 3rd year of my undergrad.

joined a team that is building a ML-driven anomaly detection system. the system is considered to be industrial-leading in the crypto space.  

worked on the backend infrastructure of the system.

## My work

delivered several big projects in my short stint:

- a root cause analysis system to analyze impacted areas caused by anomalies detected across more than 50 microservices in the company, significantly reducing the MTTR for incident managers by over 40%
- a real-time knowledge base for LLM tools to support time-sensitive Web3 domain data & internal workflow
- an alert aggregation mechanism to reduce redundant or low-signal notifications
- a better feedback mechanism aimed at improving the accuracy of the anomaly detection engine

## My takeaways

biggest takeaway is learning how to deliver fast. 
this mostly refers to the skills of understanding requirements accurately, building incrementally and set up frameworks to prevent regressions.

also thorougly enjoyed the experience of working first-hand with the users i.e. the incident managers who handles the anomalies on our system.
learned a lot on communicating with users, which is definitely very different from non-users like product managers.
