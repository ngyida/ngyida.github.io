## Context

This was my last internship during my final-year in university.

I joined the Security Engineering team that manages a wide-range of products, ranging from code-signing, IAM to Key Management.

## My work

I worked on two main projects:

### 1. Tag management for Key Management product on Bytedance's cloud-computing service https://www.volcengine.com/docs/6476/1526504?lang=en

- Engineered resource tagging capability from 0-to-1, fully compliant with the Cloud service
- Enhanced several core features with tags integration, such as access control, cost tracking & resource management
- Deployed on Bytedance's cloud-computing service, in-use by many tech companies 

### 2. Service reliability for internal code-signing tool

- Took full charge of the reliability of a new code-signing tool
- Proposed and implemented various reliability standards, includes: stress test, automated E2E test, multi-region deployment pipeline
- Drafted the official product SLI 

## My takeaways

One surprising thing that I learned about: cloud service demands rigorous requirements & compliance standards from its products.
Along with the usual complexities that comes with the matured product, the technical design was definitely challenging and fun. 

Also picked up a pseudo-engineering aspect, specifically reliability engineering.
