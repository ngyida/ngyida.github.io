## context

"Orbital", more formally known as the module CP2106, is a popular module in NUS where students carry out independent project.
This module has been using an academic portal named "Skylab" for many years.

I joined a team of freelance engineers to build "Skylab V2" that aims to replace the old portal.
My only task was to deliver the feature that I proposed and was hired for

## what I worked on

My feature aims to bridge the gap between student teams and mentors during the matching process, allowing them to browse profiles, indicate interest and participate in the mentorship matching process of multiple rounds.
To put it simply, a dating-app style matching but for academics.
I was the sole engineer for the full development process, starting from design and ending with deployment.

## what I learned

building your own idea is as fulfilling as it comes
