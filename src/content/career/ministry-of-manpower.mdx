## context

This role sat closer to operational data than to product engineering, but it gave me useful instincts that still carry into backend work today.

## what I worked on

I was part of the team overseeing Singapore's business census registry and spent time extracting, checking, and validating information from different sources.

## what mattered technically

Even without writing a large application, the work made one thing clear: data quality is a systems problem. If the underlying records are inconsistent or poorly maintained, every downstream workflow becomes less reliable.

## what I learned

This chapter made me more sensitive to correctness, source quality, and validation. Those ideas later became more concrete in backend engineering roles, but the habit started here.
