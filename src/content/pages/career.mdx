The entries below are the factual version of my career so far.
More usefully, they also contain mental notes on how each stint changed the way I think about building systems.

My recent work has lived around backend and infrastructure systems. 
That probably reflects both what I'm good at and what problem space I naturally get pulled toward.

These days, I find myself increasingly interested in the layer above pure implementation too: figuring out which ideas are actually worth pursuing, where the business value comes from, and how to tie that back into system design.
